## 📈 Statistical Validation

The following statistical tests were performed:

### ✅ ANOVA Test
- Compared sales across multiple groups.
- Evaluated whether significant differences existed.

### ✅ T-Test
- Compared two groups to determine statistical significance.

### ✅ Chi-Square Test
- Tested the relationship between categorical variables.

---

## 📊 Business Conclusion
- Sales performance varies across different customer segments.
- Product categories contribute differently to overall revenue.
- Statistical testing supports data-driven business decisions.
- Interactive dashboards improve business monitoring and reporting.
